1. As Your own Independent Financial Planning firm with your own branding.

We work with advisors that through-out the south east that wish to have their own independent office and would like Investment Planning Advisors to merely participate as their Office of Supervisory Jurisdiction (OSJ). The ramifications of the regulatory environment are pushing many advisors from a commission business model to more of a fee based model. We welcome advisors with quality character that are interested in building their own brand and a quality fee based business. Our goal is to assist advisors in transitioning their business and launching them to the next level of production with an efficient business process developed by our broker dealer (Cambridge Investment Research).
